01 readme

The engineer
behind the trace.

I'm Badri — a software engineer in the San Francisco Bay Area with 4+ years building full-stack and backend systems in Java and Python. For the last few years I've been pulling generative AI out of the demo phase and into production: multi-agent orchestration, retrieval-augmented generation, LLM evals, and MCP servers, wired into customer-facing products and held to real reliability bars.

I care about the unglamorous parts — observability, cost tracking, test coverage, on-call — because that's the line between an AI feature that demos well and one people can lean on. I think in systems first: map the structure, stress-test it, then ship.

I recently finished my M.S. in Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology, and I'm looking for a team building ambitious, AI-forward products where engineering rigor still matters.
